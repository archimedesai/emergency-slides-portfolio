Project Requirements

Emergency Presentation Service

Overview

This document outlines the specific conditions that must be met for our delivery guarantee to be valid. To ensure we can deliver high-quality presentations within the guaranteed timeframe (24-48 hours depending on package), both parties must fulfill certain obligations.

Delivery Guarantee Qualification

Our money-back guarantee applies when all the following conditions are met:

Client Requirements

  1. Complete Information Provided Upfront
    • All content, data, and materials needed for the presentation must be provided at project start
    • Company branding guidelines, logos, and color schemes (if applicable) must be provided at project start
    • Clear direction on presentation purpose, audience, and desired outcomes must be communicated
  2. Timely Communication
    • Client must respond to any clarification questions within 2 hours during business hours
    • Client must be available for a brief kick-off discussion (if requested)
    • Client must provide feedback on initial concepts within the agreed timeframe
  3. Scope Limitations
    • Content must fall within the slide count specified in the purchased package
    • Custom illustrations, animations, or videos are not included in the guaranteed timeframe
    • In-depth data analysis, research, or content creation is not included in the guaranteed timeframe
  4. Revision Limitations
    • Basic Package: 1 round of minor revisions (text edits, simple layout adjustments)
    • Standard Package: 2 rounds of revisions (includes moderate design changes)
    • Premium Package: Unlimited revisions within reasonable scope during the project timeframe

Service Provider Obligations

  1. Confirmation of Receipt
    • We will acknowledge your order within 30 minutes during business hours
    • We will confirm project scope and deadline within 1 hour of order placement
  2. Progress Updates
    • We will provide a project timeline within 2 hours of project start
    • We will share progress updates at key milestones
  3. Quality Standards
    • All slides will be professionally designed following presentation best practices
    • All text will be proofread for spelling and grammar errors
    • All designs will be optimized for your specified delivery medium (digital, print, etc.)

Business Hours & Timeframes

  • Business Hours: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time
  • After-Hours Service: Available with a 25% premium
  • Weekend Service: Available with a 50% premium
  • Delivery Timeframes:
    • Basic Package: Delivered within 24 hours from project start
    • Standard Package: Delivered within 36 hours from project start
    • Premium Package: Delivered within 48 hours from project start

Project Start Conditions

The project officially begins when ALL of the following conditions are met:

  1. Complete payment has been received
  2. All required content and materials have been provided
  3. Project brief and scope have been confirmed by both parties

Refund Policy for Guarantee Violations

If we fail to deliver your project within the guaranteed timeframe despite all client requirements being met:

  1. Basic Package: 100% refund
  2. Standard Package: 100% refund
  3. Premium Package: 100% refund

Force Majeure

Our guarantee does not apply in cases of force majeure, including but not limited to:

  • Natural disasters
  • Major internet or power outages
  • Medical emergencies
  • Other circumstances beyond reasonable control

Modifications to Project Requirements

Any changes to the project scope, content, or deadline requested after project start may void the delivery guarantee or require adjustment of the deadline and/or pricing.

Contact Information

For questions regarding these requirements or to request additional information, please use our Contact Form.
