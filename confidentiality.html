Confidentiality Policy

Emergency Presentation Service

Introduction

At Emergency Presentation Service, we understand that many of our clients entrust us with sensitive and proprietary information when creating their presentations. This Confidentiality Policy outlines our commitment to protecting your information and the measures we take to ensure confidentiality throughout our service relationship.

Scope of Confidentiality

Our confidentiality obligations extend to all non-public information provided to us in the course of our service relationship, including but not limited to:

  • Business strategies and plans
  • Financial data and projections
  • Product information and roadmaps
  • Market research and competitive analyses
  • Internal company data and metrics
  • Client and customer information
  • Proprietary methodologies and processes
  • Any information explicitly marked as confidential or that would reasonably be understood to be confidential

Our Confidentiality Obligations

We commit to the following confidentiality practices:

Protection Measures

  • Using industry-standard encryption for file transfers and storage
  • Limiting access to your information only to team members directly involved in your project
  • Requiring all team members to adhere to strict confidentiality practices
  • Implementing secure data handling procedures throughout our workflow
  • Deleting or securely archiving project files according to our data retention policy

Non-Disclosure

  • Not disclosing your confidential information to any third party without explicit prior consent
  • Not using your confidential information for any purpose other than fulfilling our contracted services
  • Not retaining your confidential information beyond the period necessary for service delivery and legal compliance

Portfolio Usage

We may request permission to include portions of our work for you in our portfolio. If granted:

  • We will only use the specific elements you approve
  • We will omit any confidential or sensitive information
  • We will honor any restrictions you specify regarding attribution or presentation

If you prefer complete confidentiality, please inform us at the start of our engagement, and we will not request portfolio usage rights.

Exceptions to Confidentiality

Our confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that:

  • Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of ours
  • Was already known to us prior to your disclosure
  • Is independently developed by us without reference to your confidential information
  • Is rightfully obtained from third parties without confidentiality obligations
  • Is required to be disclosed by law or court order (in which case we will notify you promptly unless legally prohibited)

Client Responsibilities

To help us maintain confidentiality, we ask that you:

  • Clearly mark or identify confidential information when sharing it with us
  • Provide secure methods for transferring sensitive files when possible
  • Inform us of any specific confidentiality concerns or requirements at the beginning of our engagement
  • Notify us immediately if you become aware of any confidentiality breach or concern

Term of Confidentiality

Our confidentiality obligations:

  • Begin when you first share confidential information with us
  • Continue throughout our service relationship
  • Survive the termination of our service relationship for a period of two (2) years or as required by applicable law, whichever is longer

Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a suspected or confirmed breach of confidentiality:

  • We will notify you promptly of the nature and extent of the breach
  • We will take immediate steps to remedy the situation and prevent further exposure
  • We will cooperate fully with your reasonable requests to mitigate any potential harm

Additional Confidentiality Agreements

If your organization requires a specific Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) beyond this policy, please inform us prior to sharing confidential information. We are happy to review and sign appropriate NDAs that align with standard industry practices.

Confidentiality Concerns

If you have any questions or concerns about our confidentiality practices, please contact us through our Contact Form.
